Intel Jasper Lake GT1

Intel's Jasper Lake GT1 GPU uses the Generation 11.0 architecture and is made using a 10 nm+ production process at Intel. Jasper Lake GT1 supports DirectX 12 (Feature Level 12_1). For GPU compute applications, OpenCL version 3.0 can be used. It features 256 shading units, 16 texture mapping units and 8 ROPs.

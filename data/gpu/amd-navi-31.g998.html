AMD Navi 31

Navi 31
Navi 31 XL
Navi 31 XT
Navi 31 XTX
Die Shot
Block Diagram
AMD's Navi 31 GPU uses the RDNA 3.0 architecture and is made using a 5 nm production process at TSMC. With a die size of 529 mm² and a transistor count of 57,700 million it is a very big chip. Navi 31 supports DirectX 12 Ultimate (Feature Level 12_2). For GPU compute applications, OpenCL version 2.2 can be used. Additionally, the DirectX 12 Ultimate capability guarantees support for hardware-raytracing, variable-rate shading and more, in upcoming video games. It features 6144 shading units, 384 texture mapping units and 192 ROPs. The GPU also contains 96 raytracing acceleration cores.

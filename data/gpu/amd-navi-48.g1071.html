AMD Navi 48

AMD's Navi 48 GPU uses the RDNA 4.0 architecture and is made using a 4 nm production process at TSMC. Navi 48 supports DirectX 12 Ultimate (Feature Level 12_2). For GPU compute applications, OpenCL version 2.2 can be used. Additionally, the DirectX 12 Ultimate capability guarantees support for hardware-raytracing, variable-rate shading and more, in upcoming video games. It features 3584 shading units, 224 texture mapping units and 96 ROPs. The GPU also contains 56 raytracing acceleration cores.
Further reading: RDNA Whitepaper , RDNA4 ISA

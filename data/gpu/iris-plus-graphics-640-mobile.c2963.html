The Iris Plus Graphics 640 Mobile is a mobile integrated graphics solution by Intel, launched on January 3rd, 2017. Built on the 14 nm++ process, and based on the Kaby Lake GT3e graphics processor, the device supports DirectX 12. This ensures that all modern games will run on Iris Plus Graphics 640 Mobile. It features 384 shading units, 48 texture mapping units, and 6 ROPs. The GPU is operating at a frequency of 300 MHz, which can be boosted up to 1100 MHz.
Its power draw is rated at 15 W maximum.
